<h1>United States Steam Coal Market Research Report 2025-2031</h1><br/><p>Steam Coal, also known as thermal coal, refers to coal used primarily for power generation, particularly in steam turbines to produce electricity. It is a critical component of the energy sector, especially for regions relying heavily on fossil fuels for electricity generation. Steam coal is distinguished from metallurgical coal, which is mainly used in steel production. The combustion of steam coal generates steam, which drives turbines to produce electricity.</p>
 
 
 
 <p>Steam coal is graded based on its carbon content and energy output, which determines its suitability for various applications, especially in power plants. The market is driven by the demand for energy generation, but with increasing concerns over climate change, the reliance on steam coal is gradually declining in favor of cleaner energy alternatives.</p>
