 <p><strong>Innovation Challenges</strong>: Allows organizations to set up challenges or competitions where participants can submit ideas to solve specific problems or meet business goals.</p>
 
 
 
 <p><strong>IP and Patent Management</strong>: Helps track intellectual property rights, patents, and other innovation-related legal documents.</p>
 
 
 
 <p><strong>Customizable Innovation Workflows</strong>: Provides flexibility in designing custom workflows that fit the organization&#39;s innovation strategy and culture.</p>
