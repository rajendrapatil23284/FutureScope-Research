<h1>United States Induction Furnace Power Transformers Market Research Report 2025</h1><br/><p>Induction Furnace Power Transformers (IFPT) are specialized electrical devices that are designed to supply high current at low voltage for induction heating and melting applications. These transformers play a crucial role in the metal processing industries, especially in steel manufacturing and other metal refining operations. Induction furnaces use these transformers to convert electrical energy into heat, which is essential for smelting, alloying, and refining metals. As such, they are indispensable components of modern industrial furnaces, particularly those in the steel, aluminum, and precious metal industries.</p>
