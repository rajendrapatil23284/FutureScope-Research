 <p>A rootkit is a collection of computer software, typically malicious, designed to enable access to a computer or an area of its software that is not otherwise allowed (for example, to an unauthorized user) and often masks its existence or the existence of other software. Once a system is compromised by a rootkit, the attackers are able to steal data such as passwords, gain remote access and/or even recruit the computer into a botnet. Rootkit scanner is a applicatin that can help individuals and IT managers detect and remove rootkits.</p>
