 <p>Office 365 Management Software is a category of software that helps businesses gain visibility and insights into their Microsoft Office 365 suite, which includes Microsoft Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and other email, file storage, and mobile app solutions. Office 365 Management Software helps monitor and resolve various issues within Office 365, such as service outages, and provides features such as network monitoring, workload monitoring, latency tests, licensing management, user permissions, reporting capabilities, administrative auditing, and real-time alerting. Office 365 Management Software is typically used by IT professionals to enhance the productivity, communication, and organization of their Office 365 environment.</p>
