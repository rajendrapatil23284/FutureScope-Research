<h1>Networks and Communications Security Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><br/><p>Networks and Communications Security refers to the practices, technologies, and policies designed to protect the integrity, confidentiality, and availability of data transmitted across networks. This field encompasses both wired and wireless communication channels, including local area networks (LANs), wide area networks (WANs), internet-based communications, and mobile networks. The primary goal is to mitigate cybersecurity risks such as unauthorized access, data breaches, cyberattacks, and malware intrusions.</p>
