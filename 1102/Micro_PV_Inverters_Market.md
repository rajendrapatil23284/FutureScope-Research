 <p>Micro photovoltaic inverter is a photovoltaic inverter that only works with a single solar module, converting the DC power of the solar module into AC power. Its design allows independent parallel operation of multiple micro-PV inverters in a modular fashion. The advantages of the micro photovoltaic inverter include that it can optimize the power of a single solar module, each module can operate independently, that is, the installation method is ready to use, the installation method and the improvement of fire safety, the lowest cost of system design, And the inventory can also be reduced to a minimum.</p>
