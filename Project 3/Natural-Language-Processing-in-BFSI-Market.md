<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Natural Language Processing (NLP) in the Banking, Financial Services, and Insurance (BFSI) sector refers to the application of algorithms that enable computers to understand, interpret, and generate human language data. It involves the processing and analysis of large amounts of unstructured textual data, enhancing customer interactions, automating processes, and improving decision-making within the industry.</p>

<ul>

  <li>Rising regulatory compliance requirements and the need for efficient data management solutions are prompting financial institutions to invest in NLP tools for data analysis and risk assessment.</li>
  <li>The surge in digitalization across BFSI organizations is creating opportunities for NLP applications in chatbots, virtual assistants, sentiment analysis, and fraud detection, improving operational efficiency and reducing costs.</li>
</ul>
