<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Naval Ship Maintenance, Repair, and Overhaul (MRO) refer to the activities involved in ensuring the operational readiness and longevity of naval vessels through scheduled maintenance, repair, and modernization processes. These activities are crucial for sustaining the effectiveness and safety of naval fleets.</p>

<li>Introduction of predictive maintenance techniques and IoT-enabled systems in naval ship MRO operations is streamlining maintenance processes and reducing downtime.</li>
