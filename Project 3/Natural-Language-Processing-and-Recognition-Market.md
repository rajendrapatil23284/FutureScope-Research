<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Recognition refer to the field of artificial intelligence that focuses on the interaction between computers and humans using natural language. The technology enables computers to understand, interpret, and generate human language, facilitating seamless communication between machines and users.</p>
