<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Healthcare Natural Language Processing (NLP) refers to the use of artificial intelligence and computational linguistics to analyze, understand, and generate human language in the healthcare industry. By leveraging NLP technology, healthcare providers can extract valuable insights from unstructured clinical data, improve patient care, and enhance operational efficiency.</p>

<li>Increasing demand for personalized medicine and predictive analytics in healthcare decision-making processes.</li>
<li>Emergence of voice-to-text technology for seamless documentation and improved doctor-patient interactions.</li>
</ul>
