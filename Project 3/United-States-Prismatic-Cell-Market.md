<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>A prismatic cell is a type of rechargeable battery that is used in various high-energy-density applications such as electric vehicles, portable electronic devices, and renewable energy storage systems. These cells are known for their compact design and efficient energy storage capabilities, making them a popular choice in modern consumer electronics and transportation industries. The United States Prismatic Cell Market encompasses the production, distribution, and utilization of these innovative energy storage solutions across diverse sectors.</p>
