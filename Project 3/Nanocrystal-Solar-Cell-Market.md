**Definition:**

Nanocrystal solar cells are a type of thin-film solar cell technology that utilizes nanometer-sized light-absorbing materials to enhance energy conversion efficiency. These cells offer a compact design and improved performance compared to conventional solar cells, making them an attractive option for renewable energy generation.
