<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>An Automatic Call Distributor (ACD) is a telephony software system that answers incoming calls and routes them to a specific agent or department within an organization based on pre-established criteria.</p>
<p>This technology efficiently manages call flows, reduces wait times, and ensures a seamless customer experience.</p>
