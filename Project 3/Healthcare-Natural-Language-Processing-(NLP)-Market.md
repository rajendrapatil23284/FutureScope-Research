<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Healthcare Natural Language Processing (NLP) refers to the use of advanced computational techniques to analyze and interpret human language in healthcare data. It involves the extraction of key information from clinical notes, research articles, and other sources to improve healthcare outcomes and decision-making processes.</p>
