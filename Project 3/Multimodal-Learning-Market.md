<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Multimodal learning refers to a teaching approach that integrates multiple sensory modalities, such as visual, auditory, and kinesthetic, to enhance the learning experience. This method aims to engage learners through various channels to optimize comprehension and retention of information.</p>
