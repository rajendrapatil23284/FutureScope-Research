<h2>Definition:</h2>

<p>Direct Drive Wind Turbine Generators are wind turbines that use a direct drive mechanism to transfer kinetic energy from the wind into electrical power without the use of a gearbox. This system offers simplified designs, increased efficiency, and reduced maintenance costs in comparison to traditional geared wind turbines.</p>
