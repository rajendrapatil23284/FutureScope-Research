<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Natural Language Understanding (NLU) is a subfield of artificial intelligence that focuses on the ability of machines to understand human language in the way it is spoken or written. NLU enables computers to interpret and process natural language data, allowing for more intuitive interactions between humans and machines. This technology plays a crucial role in various applications, from chatbots and virtual assistants to sentiment analysis and language translation.</p>
