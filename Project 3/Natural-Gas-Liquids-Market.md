<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Natural Gas Liquids (NGLs) refer to hydrocarbons extracted from natural gas that exist in a liquid state at standard temperatures and pressure conditions. These include butane, propane, ethane, and pentane. The versatility and various applications of NGLs make them essential components of the energy industry.</p>
